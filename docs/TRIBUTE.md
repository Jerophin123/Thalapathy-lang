# Tribute — Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (Thalapathy Vijay)

> *"Naan eppadi irundhaalum, unakku oru nanban dhaan."*
> — A fan-built language, for a leader who taught a generation to stand tall.

---

## Who this is for

**Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar** — known worldwide as **Vijay**, affectionately called **Thalapathy** by millions — is one of Tamil cinema's most enduring icons. From *Ghilli* to *Leo*, from *Master* to *Varisu*, his films gave us dialogue we repeat, characters we carry, and a sense of mass that no subtitle can fully translate.

In 2026, Vijay took office as the **Honourable Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu**, carrying the same conviction from screen to service. THALAPATHY the programming language was born in that spirit: discipline, loyalty, firepower, and the refusal to look back once committed.

This document records why the project exists. It is a **fan tribute**, written with respect — not a claim of official connection.

---

## Why a programming language?

Software is dialogue between human intent and machine behaviour. THALAPATHY chooses to speak that dialogue in the vocabulary of Vijay's world:

| Cinema root | Language echo |
|-------------|---------------|
| *Ghilli* (2004) | `ghilli` — decisive branching |
| *Pokkiri* / *Kaavalan* | `pokkiri` / `kaavalan` — try and protect |
| *Mersal* | `mersal` — function signatures with flair |
| *Master* | `master` — class definitions |
| *Varisu* | `varisu` — inheritance, lineage |
| *Bigil* / *Vaathi* | `bigil` / `vaathi` — loops that never quit |
| Fan cry *"Sollu!"* | `sollu` — tell the world (print) |
| *Thiruppi* | `thiruppi` — return what is owed |

Every module in the standard library — `sivakasi`, `vaseegara`, `kootam`, `thalaivalai` — is named after films, roles, or dialogue energy. A `.tvk` file should feel unmistakably **Vijay-native** the moment you read it.

## What THALAPATHY is — and is not

### THALAPATHY **is**

- An independent, open-source experimental programming language and toolchain (TDK).
- A celebration of Tamil cinema culture expressed through real compiler engineering.
- A place where humour, mass mode, and political-parody diagnostics stay **optional** and clearly labelled as fiction.
- Built by fans, for fans who love both code and cinema.

### THALAPATHY **is not**

- Officially affiliated with Vijay, his political party (TVK), any government body, or any film production house.
- A political platform, campaign tool, or statement of electoral fact.
- A substitute for professional news, policy analysis, or official communications.

The full legal disclaimer appears in [README.md](../README.md) and [docs/README.md](README.md).

## The name

**THALAPATHY** (தலைவpathy-adjacent fan title) honours the leader-of-the-mass identity Vijay's audience gave him long before any office. The language runtime signs bytecode with **`VJAY`**. Source files use **`.tvk`**. Compiled artifacts use **`.vijay`**. These are homages in naming — not trademarks claimed by this project.

## How to honour the tribute in your code

1. **Prefer canonical syntax** — `aama`, `illa`, `onnumilla`, `naan`, `munnadi` over legacy English aliases. Run `tdk thalapathify` to migrate.
2. **Import Vijay-native modules** — `sarkar sivakasi;` not `sarkar math;` in new projects.
3. **Use `--mode mass`** when you want diagnostics that match the energy — keep `--mode professional` for CI.
4. **Read the references** — [THALAPATHY_REFERENCES.md](THALAPATHY_REFERENCES.md) explains the filmography behind the keywords.
5. **Ship real code** — the best tribute is software that runs, tests green, and respects the disclaimer.
